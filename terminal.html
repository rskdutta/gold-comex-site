How this model reads gold

This model classifies the gold trend and drives its price forecast from the COMEX delivery structure — who delivers physical metal out (issuers) versus who stands for and takes it in (stoppers) — combined with the 10-Year US Treasury yield and the US Dollar Index (DXY) as macro context. Delivery flow shows how much physical is being claimed and how broadly; rates and the dollar set the headwind or tailwind. Together they place the tape on an accumulation ↔ distribution scale.
